Rules and Regulations

Welcome to Broad Oak Stables. We are a residential community that cares for ponies and mares of exceptional quality.

Please read and abide by these rules to make Broad Oak Stables a safe and enjoyable place for all members!

High Level Guidelines

Our fundamental governing rule is a strict "no asshole" policy. Determining whether one's status as an asshole will be at the discretion of the management, and once determined, said person banned from the parcel and all Broad Oaks associated groups.

Asshole-like behavior includes but is not limited to: ignoring stated limits; not taking "no" as an answer; insulting or personal attacks; arguing with management or other guests; causing unwanted drama; repeated and blatant disregard for the rules; intolerance; and interfering with ongoing role-play; repeated shouting in local; and/or the use of any griefer weapons or scripts.

Please do not interfere in ongoing scenes, respect the limits of others, have a good time, and contribute to the good time of others.

Broad Oak does not allow child avatars, child play, or actual children. Broad Oak is a human-based roleplay sim. Visitors are expected to follow all Broad Oak guidelines and rules. Ignorance is no excuse.

Rules
1: Agreement

By entering Broad Oak Stables, you confirm that you are 18 years or older, both in real life and as a character in Second Life, and that you have read all of the rules and guidelines, understand them, and agree to respect and abide by them and the directions of the management.

2: Minimum Avatar Age

Your avatar must be at least 60 (sixty) days old before we will even consider your application.

You are, however, free to visit and interact in Broad Oak Stables even if your avatar is younger than 60 days.

3: Zero Tolerance for Child Avatars and Inappropriate Age-Play

Any avatar that is judged to be immature in appearance will be ejected and banned immediately. Anyone playing the role of a child will be banned and reported to Linden Labs. Anyone with inappropriate child pictures or descriptions of age play in their profiles will be banned with or without notice.

The Broad Oak Stables staff reserves the right to determine what is and what is not age play, and there is no arguing or appeal.

4: 100% No Drama

Broad Oak Stables is a "No Drama Zone." Failure to follow rule, management instructions, arguing, or using abusive language will be grounds for immediate ejection and banning without recourse or appeal. In any dispute between a manager and a guest or member, the manager always wins.

5: Don't Be A Jerk

No harassment, bullying, personal attacks, or griefing. Respect limits and out-of-character requests. Bottom line — if we determine that you are being an asshole, you will be banned from Broad Oaks and its associated groups.

6: No Hate Speech and No Discussing Politics or Religion

Espousing political or religious beliefs in local or group chat is not allowed. Don't kink shame or use hate speech at anytime while you are at Broad Oak. Management's decision is final.

7: Warnings

If you are given a warning about appearance or activity at Broad Oak, you must address it immediately or be banned. Broad Oak will report any abuse of the Second Life Terms of Service to Linden Lab.

8: Wear Your Group Tags

All Broad Oak members are expected to wear their group tag while spending time at Broad Oak Stables.

9: No Vampires and No Combat

No vampire biting or fighting. Vampire RP is not welcome at Broad Oak Stables. Functional weapons — visible or hidden — are not permitted.

10: Public and Private Access

Parts of Broad Oak are private residences that are off-limits without explicit invitation. All parts may switch between public and private without warning based on management needs.

11: Fair Notice

These rules are subject to change at any time, for any reason. You are still responsible for knowing any new guidelines or rules. Ignorance is no excuse.
